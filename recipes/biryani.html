Biryani


This is a special south asian dish called Biryani

Biryani is a type of rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is made by layering rice, meat or vegetables, and a mixture of spices, and then cooking the dish with steam. Biryani is typically served with raita, a yogurt-based sauce, and is often accompanied by a spicy curry or gravy. The dish is known for its rich, flavorful taste and is a staple at many South Asian weddings and celebrations.
